FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Influencer Butler?

Influencer Butler is a desktop app for content creators that automates brand outreach, tracks commissions, and replies to DMs. It runs on Windows, auto-updates, and is built for creators in the Amazon Creator Connections ecosystem.

How do I message brands as a content creator without burning out?

The fastest way is to use a desktop tool that sends personalised pitches in bulk while keeping a safe pace. Influencer Butler writes from a template you control, rotates wording so no two brands get the same email, and queues follow-ups automatically, so you stop typing the same thing one hundred times a day.

Can I automate brand outreach for Amazon Creator Connections?

Yes. Influencer Butler reaches out to brands inside Amazon Creator Connections, follows up on your cadence, and respects per-day rate caps so your account stays healthy. You write the message once, the butler handles the sending.

Does Influencer Butler work with Gmail and Outlook?

Yes. Influencer Butler can send pitches through your own Gmail or Outlook account so replies land in your normal inbox, and it can also send through Amazon Creator Connections directly. Every email comes from you, not from a third-party sender.

Is Influencer Butler safe — will it get my account banned?

Yes, Influencer Butler is safe to use. It enforces per-day rate caps, randomised delays between messages, and message-variant rotation to keep pacing human and to stay well inside the limits of Amazon Creator Connections, Instagram, and Messenger.

How much does Influencer Butler cost?

Influencer Butler costs $29 per month, or $261 per year (a 25% saving). Every plan includes a 3-day free trial, all 29+ butlers unlocked, and you can cancel any time.

What’s the difference between Influencer Butler and a virtual assistant?

Influencer Butler runs 24/7 on your desktop for a flat $29 per month, while a virtual assistant typically costs $400 to $1,500 per month and only works the hours you pay for. The butler also never forgets a follow-up, never takes a holiday, and writes from your templates so every pitch sounds like you.

Can I use Influencer Butler on Mac?

Not yet — Influencer Butler is Windows-only today. A Mac build is on the roadmap; in the meantime, Mac creators run the Windows version through Parallels, Boot Camp, or a cheap Windows mini-PC dedicated to outreach.
