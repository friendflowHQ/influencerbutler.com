Built-In Link Shortening

Shorten Your Affiliate Links Right Inside the App

Connect your favorite link shortening service and every affiliate link gets shortened automatically during your workflow.

How It Works

1
Connect Your Provider

Add your API key from GeniusLink, URLGenius, or Linktw.in in the settings panel.

2
Run Your Workflow

Use Daily Deals, Orders Butler, or any tool that exports links. Select your link provider before running.

3
Links Get Shortened Automatically

Every Amazon URL in your export is replaced with a clean, short link from your provider. No extra steps needed.

What You Get

  • Clean, Short Links

    Replace long Amazon URLs with short, branded links for social media posts and deal shares.

  • Your Affiliate Tags Stay Intact

    Shortened links keep your affiliate tags so you still earn commissions on every click.

  • Works with Your Daily Deals & Exports

    Links shorten in the background while you harvest deals or export orders. Nothing extra to do.

  • Test Links On the Spot

    Paste any URL into the settings panel to test your connection and create links one at a time.
