Without this

Scroll Instagram for hours hunting creators in your niche. Manually copy down handles, follower counts, recent brand deals. Lose the list when your phone restarts.

With Butler

Drop in 3–5 seed creators. Butler walks the suggested-account graph, filters out the noise, and gives you a sortable table of creators with verified brand partnerships.

How it saves you time, money, and headaches

  • Find collab partners and brand-deal benchmarks in minutes, not days.
  • See exactly which brands a creator already works with — useful for pitching the same brand yourself.
  • Export the whole result set as CSV or JSON for your CRM, spreadsheet, or pitch tracker.

What’s in the box

  • Seed-and-walk discovery: paste usernames, Butler follows Instagram’s suggested-creator graph
  • Filters: min followers, min views, min comments, max posts per profile, verified-only, Reels-first
  • Disclosure filters: #ad/#sponsored hashtags, Paid-partnership labels, tagged brand mentions
  • Run modes: seeds + suggested, seeds only, suggested only
  • Two tables: Discovered accounts (with category, follower counts, source seed) and Qualifying posts (with views, likes, comments, hashtags, mentions)
  • Safe mode (2× human delays), screenshot-on-failure, manual sign-in window if Instagram challenges
  • Export to CSV / JSON; open exports folder; clear results
