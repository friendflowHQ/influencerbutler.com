Without this

You scroll Benable hoping to be noticed. Engagement is low. Real engagement — the kind that actually drives visits to your collection — takes hours a day you don’t have.

With Butler

Butler visits every list, reads each item, and posts a short, specific, on-brand comment. You set the tone, the caps, and the schedule.

How it saves you time, money, and headaches

  • Real engagement on Benable without burning your afternoons.
  • AI writes specific comments from real product details — not generic "nice list!" filler.
  • Caps + pacing + jitter keep your activity human-shaped.

What’s in the box

  • Storefronts list: paste Benable profile URLs, or import them from Benable Like Butler
  • Discover mode: scan benable.com/discover by category (Trending, Fashion, Beauty, Food, Travel, Home, Books, Health, Pets, Active Life, Wedding, Baby & Kids, Hobbies, Tech)
  • Comment text modes: AI + canned fallback, AI only, canned phrases only
  • OpenAI model picker, tone field, min/max length, custom canned phrases
  • Caps: per-run, per-day, lifetime + every-Nth-collection / every-Nth-item sampling
  • Delay + jitter between comments to mimic human pacing
  • Built-in scheduler: start time + repeat interval, with last-run + next-run pills
  • History table: comments per creator, top content tier, recent activity, CSV export
  • Default dry-run: see what Butler would post before any text goes live
